About the Project

This study investigates how climate change alters the suitability of agricultural land. By integrating climatic, soil, and topographic variables, we use Random Forest and other Machine Learning models to classify land suitability under changing climate conditions.

Our goal is to provide valuable insights for policymakers and stakeholders to adapt agricultural strategies and enhance food security.

Key Features

  • Machine Learning: Uses Random Forest, KNN, and SVM for accurate predictions.
  • Interpretabiltiy: Highlights influential factors driving suitability shifts.
  • Data-Driven: Based on comprehensive climatic, soil, and topographic datasets.
